Our Courses

Sarigama..., located at the heart of the Chennai city specialises in Music & dance classes and Orchestra. The institute is celebrated for its fine facilities, which include imported keyboards & musical instruments, state of the art practice rooms for dance equipped with quality sound systems and professional artists with over a decade experience in training. We take pride in embracing each individual's interests as our own. If you are passionate about learning Music & Dance, Our institute will be one of the best choices to learn and nurture your talent.

Temple of Fine Arts

This school is a 38-year old institution serving the field of teaching and imparting music & Bharatanatyam education. The school does this for both part-time learners and professional learners. Started in 1980, the institution first flourished under the name "Nruthya Sangita Niketan" by Guruji Natyacharya, the founder principal of this institution. The founder, Guruji Natyacharya promoting Music and Dance to his level by teaching, through research oriented books and self learning method books. Many Music and Bharatanatyam lovers are arriving to this institute from many parts of India and Abroad to learn Systematic, traditional style of Music and Dance.
