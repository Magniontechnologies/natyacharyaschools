Temple of Fine Arts

This school is a 40-year old institution serving the field of teaching and imparting music & Bharatanatyam education. The school does this for both part-time learners and professional learners. Started in 1980, the institution first flourished under the name "Nruthya Sangita Niketan" by Guruji Natyacharya, the founder principal of this institution. The founder, Guruji Natyacharya promoting Music and Dance to his level by teaching, through research oriented books and self learning method books. Many Music and Bharatanatyam lovers are arriving to this institute from many parts of India and Abroad to learn Systematic, traditional style of Music and Dance.

Expertise teaching and professional training under one roof for all styles of music and Bharathnatyam. Courses are supported with research oriented guidance through Books and CD's of Guruji Natyacharya Direct teaching of music and Bharathanatyam by Guruji with direct audio and video recordings provided with Studio quality to learned and laymen worldwide.
