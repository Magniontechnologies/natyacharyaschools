About - Course

Natyacharya’s school is offering classes and courses in all the Music and Dance subjects. Music classes are conducted weekly two days as a slot and students can take one to three slots in a week. Class duration of half-an-hour and one hour are available. The courses are targeted to a certain level in all subjects with a professional approach and training in a short time durations. All courses are certificated oriented ones and available with audio and video recordings. Courses are conducted in the following subjects like Nattuvaangam, Bharatanatyam, Art of Choreography, Dance Movements Therapy, and Dance Dramas in the dance fields. In Music, Art of Vocalization ( Voice culture course), Flute for stage and recordings, Violin techniques in Carnatic and Western style, Keyboard techniques, and programing in software, Technique of Music composing.

Flute

Guruji Natyacharya's
Flute coaching classes are available in individual method and also in Group method by direct and online ways. Guruji is giving training in Carnatic style and every students are provide with Guruji's full attention and traditional method of Flute playing. In this Natyacharya’s School of Music and Dance, Guruji teaches 8 Holes Flute known as Venu or Pullang Guzal in Carnatic style and 6 holes Flute known as Bhansuri in Hindustani style. Guruji has written the following books to learn Flute: Learn Flute in 60 days (Tamil book), Thirai Isaikku Pullanguzal Vaasikkak karru kollungal (art of playing flute for film style), Learn Flute in Indian style (a book for both Carnatic and Hindustani styles ), Learn to play Flute in 100 days (one book in English , one book in Tamil - 100 lessons Raga Alapana and Imaginary techniques), and 45 Rojulalo Venuvu Vaayicha Nerchukondi (Telugu book).
