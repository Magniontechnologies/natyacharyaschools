Vocal Music

Vocal Music

Carnatic - Traditional vocal music classes in Carnatic style are taken by Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju and Nadha abhinaya Ranjani B. Shivaranjani M.A M Phil (Music), daugter and disple of Guruji and B. Bala sushruthi Raju Vadhya kalaimani. The training start right from basics like Swaravali, Alankaras, etc. to advanced levels like varanams and krithis. Special courses available on raga exposition techniques. Also, special thematic courses like sessions on Thiruppugazh, Thiruvempavai, Thiruppavai, etc. are also available.

Hindusthani - Authentic and technically sound vocal classes are taken in Hindhusthani style also. Hindhusthani music is taught here in Gwalior Gharana. All levels of classes are covered here.

Voice culture - Special courses on voice culture are taken up here. These courses are tailor-made for each individual according to their personal needs and problems. Special trainings are given for stage singers to take up recordings, modulations, husky voice, identifing notes, mic techniques and track singing. Voice corrections are made for defective voices like the nasal dominated ones, coarse voice, etc. These special courses are taken in studio of Natyacharya's School of Music and Dance. Teaching of these courses are given to students as mp3 recordings for their home practice.

Bharathanatyam

Bharatha Natyam

Bharathanatyam - Traditional Bharatha natyam Classes are taken in Kanchivaram style. Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju is an eminent Guru teaching Bharatha natyam dance in research oriented classical method. Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju has composed and choreoghraphed many Dance items and also keeps the traditional numbers going. Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju is the disciple of Udupi Lakshmi Narayana in Bharatha natyam style. Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju teaches Bharatha natyam classes in Natyacharyas School of Music and Dance five levels to reach Ranga pravesam (arangetram).

Special provisions are available for the students who need special margam or additional margam as per their requirements in advance level. Classes for Bharatha natyam Teaching techniques and Nattuvangam is also taken by Guruji Natyacharya Sri S Balachandra Raju in systematic manner and professional method in practical way and theoretical explanations. Necessary Vocal knowledge and rhythm ( laya ) calculations will advised and taught by Guruji in the Nattuvangam courses.

Instrumental

Instrumental Music Classes

Natyacharyas School of Music encourage and giving training in basic level to advance level in all musical instruments in Carnatic style, Hindusthani style and western style in the following instruments. The classes are provide one to one method and individual training. School providing all musical instruments for learning purpose. Flute, Saxophone, Harmonica and key-flute must bring by the students.

Carnatic Style - Flute, Veena, Violin, Viola, Guitar, Mandolin, Harmonium, Jalatharangam, Clarinet, Saxophone, Key flute, Harmonica, Banjo, Bulbulthara, Muga veena, Vichithra Veena (Gottu vadhyam), Hawaain Guitar, Rudra Veena.

Hindusthani Style - Keyboard, Harmonium, Sitar, Shehnai, Bansuri, Santoor, Sarod, Esraj, Dilrupa, violin, RudraVeena.

Western Style - Keyboard, Guitar, Violin, Mandolin, Harmonica, saxophone, keyflute.

Laya Vadhya (Rhythm Instruments) - Mrudangam, Tabla, Kanjeera, Ghatam, Khole, Dolak, TablaTarang, Dolki.
