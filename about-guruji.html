About Our Teacher's

Guruji Natyacharya Sri S. Balachandra Raju is Founder and Principal of the school. Guruji is a well-known in public as a Musician, Music Director, Dancer and Dance Teacher. Guruji is also an author of 98 Music and Dance books. Guruji’s books are research oriented and very useful for laymen to research scholar in both Dance and Voice Culture, Music Composing, Flute in Carnatic or in Light style, Harmonica playing, Bharatanatyam choreography, and Dance Margam.
